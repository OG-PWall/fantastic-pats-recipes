Preppy Kitchen Sourdough

1 loaf · bread flour · adjust start time to fit your day · original ↗

Ingredients at a glance
Dough bread flour / water / starter / salt 500g / 310g / 120g / 16g
Day 1
9:00 AM
Mix
Whisk 310g water (90°F) + 120g starter until combined.
Add 500g bread flour + 16g salt. Stir until shaggy.
Cover, rest 1 hr.
10:00 AM
Stretch & fold #1
Wet hands. Pull from 12 o'clock, stretch over center, fold down. Rotate bowl 90°, repeat 3 more times (4 folds total). Cover, rest 20 min.
10:20 AM
Stretch & fold #2
Same as above. Cover, rest 20 min.
10:40 AM
Stretch & fold #3
Same as above. Cover, rest 20 min.
11:00 AM
Stretch & fold #4 — then bulk
Same as above. Shape dough into a ball, cover.
Bulk rise 6–10 hrs at 68–75°F until well puffed.
Done when a floured finger poke mostly springs back.
~9:00 PM
Shape
Stretch dough between hands and lay on counter. Flip seam-up, pinch edges toward center. Flip again seam-down, pull toward you while rotating to build tension (8–10 pulls).
Place seam-up into floured banneton or towel-lined bowl. Cover.
Rest 30 min at room temp, then refrigerate at least 1 hr (overnight is fine).
Day 2
Morning
Preheat
Dutch oven in oven at 500°F. Heat 1 full hour.
+ 1 hr
Bake — covered
Invert cold loaf onto parchment. Score. Lower into hot Dutch oven, lid on.
500°F × 20 min.
+ 20 min
Bake — uncovered
Remove lid. Reduce to 450°F × 20 min until deep golden.
Internal temp: 195–205°F.
Cool on rack at least 2 hrs before slicing.
