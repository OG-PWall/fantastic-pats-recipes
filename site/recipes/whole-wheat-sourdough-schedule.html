Whole Wheat Sourdough

1 × 920g · 78% hydration · 1:1:1 starter · Pat's whole wheat flour · adjust start time to fit your day · original ↗

Ingredients at a glance
Levain flour / water / starter 19g / 13g / 25g
Autolyse flour / water 469g / 341g
Final mix water / salt 24g / 11g
Day 1
9:00 AM
Build levain
19g flour + 13g water (80°F) + 25g starter (1:1:1, 100% hydration).
Cover, leave at 77°F.
10:30 AM
Autolyse
469g Pat's whole wheat flour + 341g water (80°F). Mix until no dry flour.
Cover, rest 90 min.
12:00 PM
Final mix
Add levain (57g), water (24g), salt (11g) to autolyse.
Squeeze and fold to combine ~5 min. Target dough temp: 75–77°F.
12:30 PM
Coil fold #1
First of 4 folds.
1:00 PM
Coil fold #2
1:30 PM
Coil fold #3
2:00 PM
Coil fold #4 — then leave it
No more touching. Bulk done when dough has grown ~50% and feels airy.
4:00 PM
Pre-shape
Shape loosely into a round.
Rest 30 min uncovered on bench.
4:30 PM
Final shape
Shape tightly into batards. Into floured bannetons seam-up.
5:00 PM
Refrigerate
Cover with plastic. Cold proof 12–18 hrs. Bake whenever you're ready tomorrow.
Day 2
Morning
Preheat
Dutch oven in oven at 500°F. Heat 1 full hour.
+ 1 hr
Bake
Score cold loaf straight from fridge.
Covered: 470°F × 22 min.
Uncovered: 470°F × 14 min.
Cool on rack 2 hrs before cutting.
