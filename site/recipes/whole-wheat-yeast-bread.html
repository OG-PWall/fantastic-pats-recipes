100% Whole Wheat Yeast Bread

1 loaf · home-milled whole wheat · same-day bake

Ingredients at a glance
Flour home-milled whole wheat 500g
Water warm, ~38°C / 100°F 375g
Salt 10g
Instant yeast 7g
Sugar or honey optional — aids browning & yeast activity 15g
  • Mix Whisk together flour, salt, and yeast (and sugar if using). Add water and mix until a shaggy dough forms with no dry flour remaining.
  • Knead Knead by hand 10–12 minutes or by machine 7–8 minutes until smooth and slightly tacky. Whole wheat won't windowpane like white flour — stop when the dough is cohesive and springs back slowly when poked.
  • First rise Cover and rest at room temperature 60–90 minutes until roughly doubled.
  • Shape Gently deflate, shape into a tight log, and place seam-side down in a greased 9×5 inch loaf tin.
  • Second rise Cover loosely and proof 45–60 minutes until the dough crowns about 2cm above the rim of the tin.
  • Bake Bake at 190°C / 375°F for 35–40 minutes. The loaf is done when the internal temperature reaches 95°C / 200°F and sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom. Cool on a wire rack at least 1 hour before slicing.
