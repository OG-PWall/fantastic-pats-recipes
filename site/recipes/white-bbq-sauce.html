White BBQ Sauce

~360g · Alabama white sauce · original ↗

Ingredients at a glance
Base mayo / white wine vinegar 232g / 60g
Flavor brown mustard / brown sugar / horseradish / lemon juice / Worcestershire / hot sauce 15g / 12g / 10g / 5g / 2g / 2g
Spice garlic powder / onion powder / cayenne / salt / pepper 2g / 2g / 1g / 3g / 1g
  • Combine Whisk all ingredients together in a bowl until smooth.
  • Rest Transfer to a sealable container. Refrigerate at least 2 hrs before serving — flavors sharpen as it sits. Keeps 1 week refrigerated.
