Pat's Whole Wheat Flour

Vitamix-milled & sifted whole wheat · yields ~500g

Ingredients at a glance
Palouse Brand Hard Red Winter Wheat Berries Certified Glyphosate Residue Free · source ↗ 500g
  • Mill Add 500g whole wheat berries to Vitamix. Blend on high until reduced to a fine powder, about 60 seconds.
  • Sift Sift milled flour into a storage container. Discard or reserve any bran that doesn't pass through.
