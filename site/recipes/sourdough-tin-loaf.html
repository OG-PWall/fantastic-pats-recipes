Sourdough Tin Loaf

1 × 1000g · 74% hydration · 1:1:1 starter · adjust start time to fit your day · original ↗

Ingredients at a glance
Levain flour / water / starter 21g / 14g / 28g
Autolyse flour / water / levain 485g / 357g / 63g
Final mix butter / honey / salt 37g / 37g / 11g
Day 1
12:30 PM
Build levain
21g flour + 14g water (80°F) + 28g starter (1:1:1, 100% hydration).
Rise 3.5–4 hrs at 77°F.
4:00 PM
Autolyse
Mix 357g water (80°F) + 63g levain (all of it), then add 485g bread flour. Mix until combined.
Rest 1 hr at 77°F. No salt, butter, or honey yet.
5:00 PM
Mix dough
1st speed 4 min. Add 37g honey + 11g salt while mixing.
Add 37g butter in pieces. 2nd speed 4–5 min.
Target dough temp: 75–77°F.
5:15 PM
Stretch #1
3 stretches total, spaced 1+ hr apart. Final stretch at least 1 hr before bulk ends.
6:15 PM
Stretch #2
7:15 PM
Stretch #3
8:45 PM
Pre-shape
Gently round into a ball. Rest 20–30 min uncovered on bench.
9:15 PM
Final shape
Shape into an extended batard to fit the tin length. Place in a greased loaf tin.
9:30 PM
Refrigerate
Rest 30 min uncovered, then cover and refrigerate overnight (12+ hrs).
Day 2
Morning
Remove from fridge
Take loaf out 1–2 hrs before baking. Preheat oven to maximum.
+ 1–2 hrs
Bake
Score a single slash down the center.
470°F × 20 min with steam.
470°F × 18 min without steam.
Cool on rack before slicing.
